Introduction to Kimiko Loci

In this blog, I reflect on animes and mangas, focusing on the Boys Love, also known as BL or Yaoi, with the genre providing a safe space for readers to explore complex emotions and relationships. The stories enable the readers to witness love that is sweet, yet resilient in the face of adversity. Consequently, what I would define as one of the strongest appeals of the BL animes and books, is how they serve as a source of comfort, offering stories about acceptance and belonging, in particular, representing emotional bonds that challenge societal norms.

Notably, these animes and manga often carry themes of growth and struggle, which invites us, the readers, to reflect on our own journeys, and how our hardships are a part of what shapes who we are. They inspire introspection into our desires, relationships and moral choices, encouraging us to contemplate what makes up our identities and our place in this world, often leading to increased self-awareness of ourselves.

Overall, the core of these stories is to experience inner conflicts and emotional turmoils through a character, and how they can overcome challenges, reminding us that growth comes from within, which is why I love unpicking BL stories!
